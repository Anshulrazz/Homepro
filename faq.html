Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you service?

We service the entire metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, insured, and undergo regular training to maintain the highest standards of service.

What are your business hours?

We operate 24/7 for emergency services. For non-emergency services, our regular business hours are Monday to Sunday, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

What types of services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of home services including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and general maintenance. Visit our Services page for a complete list.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for all our service categories. We guarantee a response within 30 minutes for emergency calls.

Do you provide warranties on your services?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on all our services and a 5-year warranty on major installations. Warranty details are provided with each service.

How are your prices determined?

Our prices are based on the type of service, complexity of the job, and materials required. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer various discounts including senior discounts, military discounts, and seasonal promotions. Check our Pricing page for current offers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and checks. We also offer financing options for larger projects.

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our website, by phone, or through our mobile app. We'll schedule a convenient time for your service.

What information do I need to provide when booking?

When booking, please provide your contact information, service address, type of service needed, and any specific details about the issue.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charges.
